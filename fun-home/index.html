About Me

My name is Verose (pronounced like "verbose" without the "b"). I graduated from MIT with a major in Humanities and Engineering, concentrating on Biological Engineering and Theater Arts. I am the first in my school with this particular combination, and it's an odd combination, but I quite like both subjects. Although I am very proud of this achievement, I Definitely Feel that people get real weird about the school name so I tend to omit that when I introduce myself in less network-y settings (it honestly works as great leverage in those areas).

Now when I introduce myself to someone, I am often at a loss as to what to say about myself. I don't want to talk about work (it's a weird thing to do I think), and I guess introductions depend on the setting, but I am still grasping for words in those moments.

Anyways, I do hope having this website will also help me sort out what my interests are, especially with what I'm proud of, that will help shape the way I say, "Hi, my name is Verose and I...".
